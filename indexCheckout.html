About

Henry. T. Lee comes from Scarborough,Ontario, 45 minutes east of Toronto, ON. He is proud of his Malaysian/Canadian heritage and looks forward to sharing their histories. He studied Technical Drafting at Ryerson University and currently enjoys urban agriculture as his traditional farming hobby to grow great gardens and to teach others, and to do his part in saving the Earth.
